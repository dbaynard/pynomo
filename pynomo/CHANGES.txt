Three basic nomograms (three variable)
--------------------------------------
	* three parallel lines 'F2(v)=F1(u)+F3(w)'
	* N nomograph 'F2(v)=F1(u)*F3(w)'
	* general determinant
              -------------------------
              | f1(u) | g1(u) | h1(u) |
              -------------------------
              | f2(v) | g2(v) | h2(v) | = 0
              -------------------------
              | f3(w) | g3(w) | h3(w) |
              -------------------------
